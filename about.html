About Us

Perla Peyzaj was established in 2025 as a fresh and dynamic company built upon the solid foundation of its founders’ 10 years of experience in the landscape design and implementation sector.

Our work focuses on providing professional solutions in both landscape design and application, combining creativity with technical expertise to deliver high-quality and lasting outdoor spaces.

We offer a wide range of ornamental plants, both locally grown and imported from leading European suppliers in Italy, Germany, the Netherlands, Belgium, Spain, Hungary, and Moldova. Our clients can easily access high-quality plants of all sizes directly from our 10,000 m² nursery located in Büyükçekmece.

At Perla Peyzaj, our mission is to enhance every project with professionalism, innovation, and reliability. With a dedicated and knowledgeable team, we are committed to creating sustainable, aesthetic, and functional green spaces that bring long-term satisfaction to our clients.
